Sludge Soda

Sludge soda is a fake soda brand i created as part of a project in my Graphic Print Production class. The crux of the brand is that it is a soda that is honest about its self, rather than express itself as anything other than something that is terrible for you. The design for this project was inspired by warning signs and toxic substance containers and tried to carry that theme taught to have a tongue and cheek reference to the negative effects of soda on the body.
