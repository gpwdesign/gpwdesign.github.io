Light Polution Project

This project was focussed on teaching us how to express abstract ideas through both visual and verbal languages. Our four posters were meant to operate as a gradient going from all verbal communication to all visual communication. My subject for the project was light pollution. I expressed it by taking elements of a city lights from an overhead perspective and making them muddy and gross to get the feeling of unnaturally and harm. This project was featured in the juried student art show at Champlain colleges in 2019.
