On The Water Internship

My time at On The Water Media was an eye opening experience when it comes to design. It was the first time that any of my design work would be used in a professional, and I definitely felt the pressure from that. The experience I have gained and the lessons that I learned their were indispensable, everything to how to troubleshoot a printer to how to work in a professional setting everything was indispensable.
